Greenling

Greenling is one of those community benefactors. They want to change the world. Their mission: build sustainable communities by creating a support system for local small farmers, cutting energy expenditures, and delivering high quality organic foods to new customers daily. Not just local foods, organic foods that are herbicide and pesticide free, grown within 100 miles of you, and delivered fresh to your door on a regular basis. Greenling does more than carpool groceries. By helping small farms get their goods to nearby consumers almost the day they are picked, plucked, or pulled from the ground, Greenling supports local economies where 'Small is Beautiful'. Assessing Parenting

AssessingParenting, a national parent-child network designed to prevent child abuse, is changing the world for millions of people. The inventory is a diagnostic tool used to assess the risk of unsafe parenting behaviors. With this simple online form family service agents can quickly gauge healthy family dynamics and prevent the maltreatment of children. The inventory is currently available in English and Spanish, though it will ultimately be translated into 37 languages, with Arabic, Creole, Japanese, and Korean currently underway.

Himalayan Institute

The Himalayan Institute is another organization changing the world for the better. Founded by Swami Rama of the Himalayas, the Institute is an established humanitarian center with world-wide membership, volunteer services, workshops and other educational opportunities designed to “discover and embrace the sacred link—the spirit of human heritage that unites East and West, spirituality and science, and ancient wisdom and modern technology.” With an emphasis on rural empowerment, the humanitarian works of the Himalayan Institute include building a Community Center in Kumbo, Cameroon, funding schools in India, and sponsoring biodiesel energy farming projects in India and Africa.
