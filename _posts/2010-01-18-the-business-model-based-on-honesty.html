Trust is at the cornerstone of relationships, busines included.

Honesty Model: There is a store that exists on the bottom of my mountain. It supplies general packaged foods and local, produce, most of which is organic. The kicker is the store follows an Honor System, that means there is no clerk there, you go in and they Trust that you will document your food, calculate the total with tax, and leave a total.

Good Food Good People

For such a small store, it gets a fair amount of traffic.
