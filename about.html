Core Value

Our core value is to use Entryway's considerable talent to build sustainable businesses that are economically, environmentally, and socially responsible.

Businesses like:

Greenling

Greenling wants to change the world. Their mission: build sustainable communities by creating a support system for local small farmers, cutting energy expenditures, and delivering high quality organic foods to new customers daily. Not just local foods, organic foods that are herbicide and pesticide free, grown within 100 miles of you, and delivered fresh to your door on a regular basis. Greenling does more than carpool groceries. By helping small farms get their goods to nearby consumers almost the day they are picked, plucked, or pulled from the ground, Greenling supports local economies where 'Small is Beautiful'.

Himalayan Institute

The Himalayan Institute is an organization changing the world for the better. Founded by Swami Rama of the Himalayas, the Institute is an established humanitarian center with world-wide membership, volunteer services, workshthe spirit of human heritage that unites East and West, spirituality and science, and ancient wisdom and modern technology.” With an emphasis on rural empowerment, the humanitarian works of the Himalayan Institute include building a Community Center in Kumbo, Cameroon, funding schools in India, and sponsoring biodiesel energy farming projects in India and Africa.
Watch a video about Himalayan Humanitarian efforts >>

Family Development Resources Inc.

Assessing Parenting, a national parent-child network designed to prevent child abuse, improving the lives of millions of people. Using a diagnostic tool they assess the risk of unsafe parenting behaviors. With this simple online form family service agents can quickly gauge healthy family dynamics and prevent the mistreatment of children. The diagnostic tool is currently available in English, Arabic, Creole, and Spanish; ultimately it will be translated into 37 languages.
