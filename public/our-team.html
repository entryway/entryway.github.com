Our Team

the men and women behind your dream

Experience Determines Project Success

Entryway is a small and focused team by design. We feel the intentional size of our core team enables us to have a closer relationship with our clients, while delivering more effective and innovative solutions than a large, complex firm is able to.
 

Gustin Prudner

Creating software with cutting-edge tools, building projects, and working with great clients makes my days spin. I have a multi-disciplinary background and a myriad of interests. I left a position as Director of Enterprise Architecture at a large international firm to help form Entryway. The single premise of Entryway is to wield technology in the support of good things both fun and world changing. Working with our team of like-minded folks at Entryway is an added delight.

Outside the virutal sphere I enjoy tromping around my land, celebrating nature with my family and newly hatched son -- Loic.

Patrick Veverka

I represent Entryway at our Hampton Roads office. I am an energetic senior software professional with a decade of experience implementing the software needs of a variety of businesses. I believe that good developers write solid algorithms. Language is merely a tool to manifest those algorithms. As such, my focus is on perfectly written algorithms in Microsoft and open source technologies, such as PHP, .NET, and Ruby on Rails. Customizing solutions for our clients is what I do best.

In my free time I enjoy sports and spending time with my wife and two small children.

Jonathan Greenberg

I have long dreamt of becoming a rurally based programmer and gardener. Entryway has made this dream a reality. I enjoy Ruby/Rails and the NET framework. In my spare time I take great joy from gardening, playing chess, and reading bedtime stories to my son, Gavin. My family and I live in a small homesteading intentional community in Floyd, Virginia, where we are working to build our own home.

Chris Angileri

My first love came to me at age ten: a Commodore 64. I couldn't wait to take the machine apart and find out how it all worked. I have served as an IT professional at various large companies in the many years since, but no place has ever felt as productive and successful as the team here at Entryway. This small, highly efficient outfit is the most cutting edge team I've ever been involved with. The emphasis here is on creativity and personal development, which allows me to focus on what I'm here for: systems engineering. This frees me up to spend time producing music and celebrating my newest love: my newborn daughter, Maya-sun.
