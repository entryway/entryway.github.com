working with us

Agile Development

At Entryway we are dedicated to use a flexible, agile process that is mutually beneficial and transparent. It combines Test-Driven Development, Pair Programming, Short Iterations, and Open Communication, to produce a process has not only improved our quality of work, but it has helped clients prioritize and stay within their budget.

Ruby on Rails

The most powerful and flexible language available on a platform that out performs in all arenas, Ruby on Rails has been the power behind our problem solving. We are also active in the Ruby community using and contributing to open source technology and hosting and attending code retreats to stay up date on this fast moving technology.

who we are

We nurture good businesses

Our core value is to use Entryway's considerable talent to build sustainable businesses that are economically, environmentally, and socially responsible. We feel that the quality of our work is reflected best in the quality of the organizations we serve. Good projects need nurturing. We believe that Entryway provides foundational support, and that the work we do nurtures organizations that become strong benefactors of the communities they serve.
